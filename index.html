Mysore Palace

Situated in the southern state of Karnataka, it used to be the official residence of the Wodeyar Dynasty, the rulers of Mysore from 1399 to 1950. The grand palace stands tall in the heart of Mysore city and attracts visitors from across the world. Thanjavur Temple

Thanjavur Temple, also known as Brihadeeswara Temple, is one of the most magnificent and largest temples in India. Located in the southern state of Tamil Nadu, it was built by Raja Raja Chola I in the 11th century and stands as a masterpiece of Chola architecture. The grand temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is renowned for its towering vimana, intricate carvings, and massive stone sculptures. Situated in the heart of Thanjavur city, it attracts devotees and tourists from all over the world. As a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in history, architecture, and culture.
